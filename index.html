P/02 · NETWORKING

Wind Turbine to Space Station Protocol

January 2026 – May 2026

A custom binary networking protocol built from scratch over raw UDP: 9 byte fixed header, five message types, checksum verification, and stop and wait ARQ. Runs as a three node distributed system across physically separate machines simulating a LEO satellite relay with configurable propagation delay, packet loss, and blackout windows. NACK triggered retransmission handles corruption.
